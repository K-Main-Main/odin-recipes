Toum

Toum is a amazing side to almost anything. bread? you bet, a sandwich forget about it, a side piece of a charcuterie board you've made magic happen. I had Toum for the first time in Colorado at restaurant called El Five in Denver Colorado. Its is the simple but has this unique depth of flavor that i can only describe as magic.
