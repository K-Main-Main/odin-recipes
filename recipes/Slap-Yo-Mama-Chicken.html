Slap Yo Mama Chicken

Slap Yo Mama Chicken is something of my own creation at least this version of it. At its core it is a cilantro Chicken and lime. I made this one day with the random stuff that i had left over and it turned out so good i could have slapped my Mama but now really though she would have sent me to a grave but the thought was there.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 1 bunch of cilantro
  • 1 medium red onion
  • 1/4 of a cup of lime juice
  • 1/2 tbs of worchesire sauce
  • 1/2 tbs of soy sauce
  • 1/2 tbs garlic powder
  • 1 tps of salt
  • 1/2 tps of cumin
  • 1/2 of curry powder
  • High smoke point oil

Lets slap the Mama...not really though

  1. your chicken and pat them with a paper towel until dry. once dry place chicken in a medium sized mixing bowl

  2. dice the whole red onion into small pieces and place them in the bowl with the chicken.

  3. remove cilantro leaves from the stem and chop the leaves until fine. once chopped place in the bowl with the chicken.

  4. pour the lime juice, soy sauce and worchesire sauce into the bowl with the chicken. with your hands mix all the Ingredients in the bowl until fully combined. let this sit in the refrigerator for about 20 minutes.

  5. after 20 minutes add your garlic powder,cumin,curry powder and salt to the mixture. use your hands until seasonings are fully combined. places back in refrigerator another hour.

  6. while waiting for the chicken take a medium size sheet tray and pre-grease it with a high smoke point oil like avocado oil.

  7. once ready place chicken on the sheet tray and place it in oven at 400 degrees. cook chicken on one side for 20 minutes and flip the chicken over and cook on the other side for 20 minutes. Side note when placing chicken on the sheet tray make sure to scrape the bowl for any leftovers and place that on top of the chicken.

  8. Take the chicken out of the oven. let sit for 10 minutes and enjoy Slapping Yo Mama...not really though.

  9. For real Do Not Slap Your Mom!
